Request Tracking

Management System

The Request Tracker Management System is designed to manage requests efficiently through an integrated Correspondents tracking system, enabling smooth communication and coordination among users. It provides effective monitoring of the procurement process, ensuring timely progress at every stage. The system offers secure and easy access to work completion data for authorized officers. With its user-friendly interface, users can navigate and perform tasks with ease. Additionally, the system includes automated email notifications to inform completion officers and relevant officers of updates, ensuring prompt action and improved workflow management
